Advanced Terrain Texture Splatting

This package allows you to reach close to a natural terrain image.
It uses our know-how algorithms described on http://u.bretwalda.net/12vDVZi.

Features:
- Close to natural blending result.
- Fully compatible with Unity Terrain.
- Tool for preparing Diffuse maps with Depth.

Usage:
Simply create material with the "Nature/Terrain/Advanced Diffuse" shader and apply it to Terrain.
You can use "Window/Advanced Terrain Texture Splatting/Create Diffuse with Depth..." menu item to create Duffuse maps with Depth.
